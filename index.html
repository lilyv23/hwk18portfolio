Get to Know Me

I’m a Latina that enjoys creative thinking and coming up with solutions, which is why I recently became a freelance UX Designer. I find it fascinating learning about the struggles that users encounter using everyday web content, apps, systems, etc. and how we can improve those experiences. I am a process oriented individual who likes to identify risks and impacts to develop the best possible solution, but this does not mean I do not thrive gray areas. Planning and creating contingencies are my expertise.

My Experience

For the last 8 years, I've spent my time in business process development. Working at one of the leading innovative financial institutions, I want to be part of the design and development process for some of the top applications consumers come across and truly make a difference that matters.

My Skills

The ability to work in a team is imperative in the design field. I have been part of many teams and lead them to successful launches due to the collaboration and consistent communication amongst each other. I am a self-starter and like to take initiative with projects. I've learned that being diplomatic and understanding others thought process is extremely valuable.
